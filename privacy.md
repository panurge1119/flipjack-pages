# FlipJack Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 21, 2026_

FlipJack respects your privacy.

## Overview

FlipJack is a split-flap music display app for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## Data Collection

FlipJack does not sell your personal information.

FlipJack does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

FlipJack does not use third-party analytics SDKs, unless otherwise stated in a future app update.

## Information You Provide

If you contact support by email, I may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address and the contents of your request.

## App Functionality Data

FlipJack may access limited device or app state information required for the app to function properly. Any such access is used only to provide the app’s features.

## Data Sharing

I do not share your personal information with third parties except when necessary to:
- respond to your support request
- comply with legal obligations
- protect the safety, security, or integrity of the app or users

## Data Retention

If you email support, I may retain that correspondence for as long as reasonably necessary to respond and maintain support records.

## Children’s Privacy

FlipJack is not directed to children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page, with the updated effective date above.
